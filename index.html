Lab.I.O. - Laboratory for Interaction and Orality Lab.I.O. - Laboratory for Interaction and Orality

Lab.I.O. is an ongoing project to create performance and interactive installations based on orality and sound. Its projects have been run by Mick Mengucci with the contribution of many collaborators and friends.

LooperAcc This is a handy loop station for mobile devices (smart phones, tablets)

Download the LooperAcc.pd and LooperAcc.mmp files to your device with MobMuPlat installed.

Open MobMuPlat and look for LooperAcc.mmp, click o n it and play with it.

The .pd file is the engine in Pure Data, the .mmp file is the mobile interface.

Mick Mengucci

Engineer, musician, perfomer and human.

Italian, resident in Lisbon, since 1998, he mixes scientific skills with his music,

poetry and art. He is a professional guitarist and singer, multimedia teacher

at ETIC-EPI and event organizer. His focus is on creating facilities, apps,

installations and performance that involve spoken word, video with and multimedia interaction.

Coordinator and founder of Lab.I.O. in collaboration with other artists and activists.

He gives training workshops in poetry slam and in digital interactivity.
