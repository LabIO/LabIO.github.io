Lab.I.O. - Laboratory for Interaction and Orality Lab.I.O. - Laboratory for Interaction and Orality

Lab.I.O. is an ongoing project to create performance and interactive installations based on orality and sound. Its projects have been run by Mick Mengucci with the contribution of many collaborators and friends.

INSTALLATIONS

EXPAND 2019 During the Expand artistic residency at the headquarters of the association Espaço do Tempo, Montemor, an interactive installation based on SonarX real-time image sonification software, designed by me with colleague Francisco Medeiros, was designed and displayed. video

NEXTSTOP/LXPOETA 2019 As part of the Largo Residências cutural cooperative project, a video installation was created in the northern atrium of the Anjos station. An ultrasonic distance sensor detects the passage of each person and shoots a 5sec video. In the middle of the lobby, an infrared presence sensor activates videos of larger contents, which to be seen until the end requires the person to stir from time to time (as with bathroom light). The system runs on a Raspberry Pi 3B with an Arduino Uno and automatically restarts when the power is turned on without requiring any instruction from those responsible for the festival.https://www.largoresidencias.com/projectos/sou-lxpoeta

SYNESTHEATRE 2016 As a presentation of the device developed by the Department of Synesthesia, University of Sussex, an installation was created that allowed users to try it out and verify its usefulness in identifying colored objects in space.

CATRAIA TOTEM 2019 Project presented in the first edition of the Catraia Festival, Praia da Tocha, Aveiro. A structure of recycled beach cleaning materials supports a system of 6 distance sensors connected by Arduino that control a lighting system of a 5m strip of LEDs.video 1 BARULHÓMETRO 2012-2020 Este trabalho é um projeto em contínuo desenvolvimento como sistema de suporte multimédia para eventos de Poetry Slam. Consiste num display generado pelo Raspberry Pi com Pure Data e controlado via browser, com várias funções entre as quais de captar o barulho do público e converte-lo numa nota de 0 a 10.

DOWNLOADS

LooperAcc This is a handy loop station for mobile devices (smart phones, tablets) that records from your microfone to 2 tracks where you can add effects on and control them through sliders in the interface and also through the position of your device.

How to install it? Download the LooperAcc.pd and LooperAcc.mmp files to your device with MobMuPlat installed. Open MobMuPlat and look for LooperAcc.mmp, click on it and play with it. The .pd file is the engine in Pure Data, the .mmp file is the mobile interface.

Mick Mengucci

Engineer, musician, perfomer and human. Italian, resident in Lisbon, since 1998, he mixes scientific skills with his music, poetry and art. He is a professional guitarist and singer, multimedia teacher at ETIC-EPI and event organizer. His focus is on creating facilities, apps, installations and performance that involve spoken word, video with and multimedia interaction. Coordinator and founder of Lab.I.O. in collaboration with other artists and activists. He gives training workshops in poetry slam and in digital interactivity.
